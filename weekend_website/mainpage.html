   Tuning is an upgrade that unlocks hidden potential of your car.
Car tuning is the modification of a car to optimise it for a different set
of performance requirements from those it was originally designed to meet.
Most commonly this is higher engine performance and dynamic handling
characteristics but cars may also be altered to provide better fuel economy,
or smoother response. The goal when tuning is the improvement of a vehicle's
overall performance in response to the user's needs. Often, tuning is done
at the expense of emissions performance, component reliability and occupant comfort.
